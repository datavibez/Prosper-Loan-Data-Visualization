Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the features of loans in the prosper loan data set that could be used to predict the Borrower Annual Percentage Rate.My main focus was on the original loan amount,borrower's rate,Employment Status, borrower's Prosper rating, borrower's Prosper score,loan Term,Lender Yield,income range and estimated return.

Dataset Overview¶

The Prosper Loan Dataset explores a dataset containing 113,937 loans(rows) with 81 variables (columns )on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, borrower employment status, borrower credit history, and the latest payment information.
